Pickled Onions

Description

Have you ever gone to a restaurant, seen pickled onions on a dish you ordered and thought, "Wow these are delicious! They seem quite fancy though, probably difficult to make myself".

Well you are in luck! Pickled onions are unbelievably easy to make and add a nice tang to any dish, elevating it to restaurant-quality instantly!

Ingredients

Makes 1 Jar

  • 2 medium red onions, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 250ml white wine vinegar
  • 200ml boiled water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp peppercorns

Steps

  1. Add the sliced onions into a jar.
  2. Add the boiled water and vinegar to a heatproof measuring cup and mix well.
    • Tip: Alternatively you can boil this mixture in a pan.
  3. Mix in the sugar and salt into the water-vinegar mixture and stir until dissolved.
  4. Add the peppercorns to the jar with the onions.
  5. Pour the water-vinegar mixture over the onions making sure the onions are completely submerged.
  6. Close the lid and give the jar a good shake so that everything is thoroughly mixed.
  7. Leave the jar to sit for at least 15 minutes. Once it reaches room temperature, I recommend putting the jar in the fridge overnight to allow the onions to infuse.
  8. Use the onions as a garnish and enjoy!
